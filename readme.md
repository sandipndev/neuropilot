# NeuroPilot
> Your AI co-pilot for focus

NeuroPilot helps you stay focused and remember what you explore online. It quietly follows what you read or look at, understands where your attention lingers, and captures the themes youâ€™ve been learning about. When your mind starts to wander, it offers small, friendly nudges or short recall questions to bring you back. Over time, NeuroPilot builds a simple trail of your interests and learning progress - turning scattered browsing into a clear, connected journey of ideas.

## Features

User Activity:
- Track what the user is looking/reading about
- If image, generate caption

Attention:
- We track activity to understand sustained attention on an image/readable text

Focus:
- One or two worded broad classification about the Attention over a longer period of time

Focus Drift:
- Checking if new Attention info is related to one's previous Focus

Attention Summary:
- Summary of accumulation of Attention over a small period of time

Pulse:
- Short personalized learning progress updates to nudge user to stay focused

Quiz:
- Questions based on Attention and Focus
- 2 options / one correct
